HEARING AIDS CARE

Some helpful tips for patients and to get most out of your hearing aid devices.

 

  • Avoid moisture and store in a dry place.
  • Keep away from water.
  • Keep away from pets.
  • Change hearing aid batteries often.
  • Change your wax filter.
  • Schedule routine cleaning.
  • During night open the battery door and keep the hearing aid in the white box.
