Child Hearing Testing

Newborn hearing screening identifies most children born with a hearing loss. But in some cases, the hearing loss is caused by things like infections, trauma, and damaging noise levels, and the problem doesn't emerge until later in childhood. Researchers believe that the number of people who have hearing loss doubles between birth and the teen years.
